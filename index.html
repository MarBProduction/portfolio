Booking project

Backend - Frontend - API

This website was a group project doing my Web Development bachelor degree. The goal was to create a booking solution, to book rooms at our school. The booking should be saved in our database and linked to the users account, so the user could view, change or delete their bookings. Double bookings should also be avoided.

The focus was on backend development, so it may not be the prettiest solution, but it works.

Check it out!

Dating site project

Backend - Frontend - API

This website was another group project doing my Web Development bachelor degree. The goal was to create a dating website solution, where you could make a profile, and choose your dating preferences. The user should then be suggested dates based on their preferences. Additionally it was required to cover every CRUD element.

Since backend development was the focus and design not important, we had some fun with the design choices. Do not pay too much attention to the design, since it is not a representation of how I would design a serius dating site.

Check it out!

Lollitop

B2B - UX Design - Frontend

This website was a group project doing my Multimedia Design education. The goal was to design an informativ website for the small cloth brand Lollitop8000, including a design for a webshop, however not the functionality for one.

Their brand was committed to a very extreme Y2K style, which isn't the most UX and UI friendly style. So we worked with the owners of the brand to implement the style as much as we could in our solution, without hurting the UX.

Check it out!

Bonus video: Gamification

Disclaimer: The video is in danish, but might still be worth a watch, even if you do not understand danish

Doing my 2. semester of my Multimedia Design education, we were tasked to make a video, where we should present a topic. The topic I chose was Gamification, which is using video game elements in your products to keep the user engaged.

This video took a little less than a week to make and got a lot of praise from my classmates as well as my teacher, who all found the video entertaining and funny. So, the video has made it into my portfolio, since it shows a bit of my personality, humour, and my skills as a presenter and video editor. Enjoy.

About Me

I have always been interested in the digital world, and the endeless ways to make everyday life easier and more entertaining.

First, I tried my hands on the art of graphic design, but those hands were not cooperating. Then doing my Multimedia Design education I focused on the coding, which worked better for me, as I started to enjoy it more and more, it just feels great to make ideas come to life.

The more I learn, the more I want to know, as I realize just how much more I have yet to learn. I love diving into topics, and try to understand what happens behind the scenes. Because I learn the best when I can draw from the experiences of others and try it out myself. For the same reasons, I love to share my knowledge with others, when working in a team.

I am a detail-oriented and dedicated worker, who try to make everything right. If the solution is not smooth and easy to understand for the consumer, it can be improved. So, if you want someone who takes thier job serious, with a good work ehtic, but still have time for fun, let us get in touch.
